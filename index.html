Privacy Policy

App Name: FocusZone

This privacy policy explains how FocusZone handles user and device data to ensure transparency and compliance with Google Play policies.

Data Collection

FocusZone uses third-party services to enhance user experience and serve ads. The following types of data may be collected automatically and anonymously:

  • Ad interaction data (via Google AdMob SDK)
  • Device model, operating system version, and app usage duration

Purpose of Data Use

All collected data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Improving app functionality
  • Serving optimized ads
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience

No personal data such as name, email, or contacts is collected or stored.

Third-Party Services

FocusZone integrates the Google AdMob SDK to serve interstitial and rewarded ads. These services may use anonymized data for ad personalization or frequency capping based on your consent settings.

Data Security

We are committed to protecting user data. All collected information is processed anonymously and is not stored on any external server or database.

Children's Privacy

FocusZone is suitable for all ages, including children under the age of 13. No data that personally identifies a child is collected. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. All updates are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
